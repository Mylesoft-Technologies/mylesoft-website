Who We Are

Built for Africa.
Powered by AI.

MylesCorp Technologies Ltd was founded in Nairobi, Kenya with one clear purpose: to harness the power of artificial intelligence to solve Africa's most pressing challenges. We are not adapting foreign technology — we are building native solutions.

Every product integrates with M-Pesa, supports Kenyan regulatory requirements, and is backed by a dedicated support team based right here in Nairobi.
